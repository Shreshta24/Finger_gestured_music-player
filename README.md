### Project Description
A Carnatic music player that uses hand gestures to play different swaras. No mouse, no keyboard ‚Äî just flex your fingers and let the ragas flow.

### The Problem (that doesn't exist)
Do you ever feel like your fingers are too idle while listening to music?
Ever wanted to compose a raga while flexing like a gym bro?

### The Solution (that nobody asked for)
We made a system where your hand muscles control music.
Raise a finger, hear a swara. Raise all five? It's practically a concert.
No actual musical knowledge required ‚Äî just working joints.
